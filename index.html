About the Challenge

Focusing on multilingual medical conversational speech between community health workers and patients

The DISPLACE 2026 challenge aims to advance diarization and speech understanding technologies in real-world healthcare conversations. The dataset includes multilingual, code-switched interactions between Accredited Social Health Activists (ASHAs) and patients, representing authentic primary healthcare settings.

Challenge Tracks

Three core tracks for 2026

Track 1 — Speaker Diarization (SD)

Determine “who spoke when” in code-mixed multilingual medical dialogues with overlapping speech.

Track 2 — Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR)

Transcribe multilingual, code-switched healthcare conversations across dialects and noisy environments.

Track 3 — Topic Identification

Identify conversation topics (e.g., maternal health, infection)

Track 4 — Summarization

Produce concise medical dialogue summaries.

Database

Multilingual medical dialogue corpus

Corpus Composition

~30 hours of real primary-healthcare interactions recorded between community health workers (ASHAs) and patients in local clinics and homes.

Languages & Code-Mixing

Hindi, Kannada naturally mixed across turns, reflecting true multilingual usage.

Recording Setup

Single-channel 16 kHz 16-bit WAV recordings, far-field microphones, real acoustic conditions, annotated for speaker segments, language, and overlap.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which languages can I use for development?

Any language is allowed; outputs must be Rich Transcription Time Marked (RTTM) files for evaluation.

How do I get the DISPLACE dataset?

Register for the challenge and send the signed agreement to displace2026@gmail.com to receive access credentials.

Can I share or redistribute the data?

No, redistribution is prohibited. You may use it for research with proper citation.

In which format should I submit outputs?

Submit RTTM text files with diarization or recognition outputs per the evaluation plan.

How can I submit my work to INTERSPEECH 2026?

Follow the INTERSPEECH 2026 paper submission portal and select “DISPLACE Challenge” when uploading.
